Ingredients

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 stick of butter (1/2 cup)
  • 2 T tarragon vinegar
  • 1 t salt

Equipment

  • Blender
  • Sauce pan
  • Bowls

Directions

  1. Heat butter in sauce pan until slightly hot.
  2. Pour egg yolks into blender.
  3. Add tarragon vinegar and salt.
  4. Cover blender and turn to low speed.
  5. Drizzle in butter slowly while covering with paper towel.
  6. Serve sauce with steak and vegetables.
